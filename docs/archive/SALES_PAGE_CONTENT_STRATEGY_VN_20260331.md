## 🎓 Content Writing Principles Used

### 1. **Problem-Solution Framework**
- Start with pain (Excel, calls, confusion)
- Show solution (automation, real-time, clarity)
- End with benefit (save money, time, stress)

### 2. **Vietnamese Cultural Context**
- Use local terminology (BKS, cầu đường, bãi, khách hàng)
- Reference real Vietnamese logistics challenges
- Show respect for transparency and fairness (công bằng)

### 3. **Numbers & Specifics**
- "20-30 giờ/tháng" not "lots of hours"
- "15-20% waste" not "save money"
- "6 problems" shows depth of understanding
- "30% minimum savings" is credible vs vague promises

### 4. **Practical Features**
- Focus on jobs-to-be-done (calculate salary, track fuel, get reports)
- Show concrete workflows (app → driver → photo)
- Include export options (Excel cho kế toán = practical Vietnamese need)

### 5. **Emotional Language**
- "Không còn gọi điện" (no more calls!) = relief
- "Công bằng & minh bạch" (fair & transparent) = trust
- "Không quên hạn" (never forget deadline) = confidence
