# USP Sales Pack - FleetPro Web (Practical)

## 1) Core USP (1 sentence)
FleetPro giup doanh nghiep van tai giam tre dieu phoi, giam that thoat chung tu va chot duoc dau vet van hanh theo thoi gian thuc tren mobile.

## 2) 5 USP de ban hang (khong mau me)
1. Mobile-first cho tai xe:
- Check-in GPS, checklist xe, gui anh/video ngay tren phone.

2. 4-role workflow co that:
- Tai xe, Dieu phoi, Ke toan, Quan ly phoi hop theo 4 buoc ro rang.

3. Audit trail day du:
- Moi hanh dong quan trong co log + timestamp + actor + trang thai.

4. Telegram ops real-time:
- Bao cao text/anh/video day vao kenh chung, giam tre thong tin noi bo.

5. Trien khai nhanh chi phi thap:
- Dung stack free-tier de pilot va scale tu tu theo doanh thu.

## 3) Pain -> Value (ban hang theo noi dau)
- Pain: Dieu phoi tre, tai xe bao cao manh mun.
  Value: Luong 4 buoc + notify ngay trong Telegram, xu ly trong phut.

- Pain: Chung tu chi phi that lac, doi soat cham.
  Value: Tai xe gui chung tu tai hien truong, ke toan doi soat theo chuyen.

- Pain: Khong truy vet duoc ai lam gi.
  Value: Timeline log hop nhat, co bang chung khi can kiem tra.

## 4) 60-second sales script
"Anh chi dang mat tien vi 3 diem: dieu phoi tre, chung tu roi rac, va khong co dau vet ro ai lam gi. FleetPro gop 4 vai tro vao 1 luong cong viec ro rang tren mobile: tai xe check-in GPS + checklist + media, dieu phoi ra lenh va theo doi, ke toan doi soat theo chuyen, quan ly chot ngay. Toan bo su kien day ve kenh Telegram va log he thong, nen minh nhin thay ngay van de va xu ly trong phut, khong doi den cuoi thang moi biet lo." 

## 5) Offer goi de chot pilot tra phi
- Pilot 14 ngay co phi (muc nho) cho 1 doi xe.
- Cam ket KPI pilot:
  - Ty le xac nhan lenh < 3 phut
  - Ty le checklist truoc chuyen > 90%
  - Ty le doi soat trong 24h > 90%
- Neu khong dat KPI da cam ket: gia han pilot them 7 ngay khong tinh phi.

## 6) CTA mau
- "Cho toi 1 doi xe pilot 14 ngay, toi se cho anh chi thay dashboard KPI truoc/sau bang so lieu that."
- "Neu sau 14 ngay anh chi khong thay cai thien toc do dieu phoi va doi soat, minh dung tai day."
